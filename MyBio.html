Ralph's Recipe for a ham and cheese sandwhich.

  1. Get yourself 2 slices of bread, mayo, mustard, roman lettuce, slice tomato, slice pickles, smoked ham, and gouda cheese.
  2. Spread with a knife mayo on one slice and mustard on the other slice of bread.
  3. Layer ham, gouda, lettuce, tomato, and pickles.
  4. Cut sandwhich in half and enjoy:)
